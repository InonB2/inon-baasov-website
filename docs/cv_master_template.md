## VISUAL FORMAT RULES

- Font: Clean sans-serif (as per V1 PPM)
- Name: Large centered header — **INON BAASOV**
- Contact line: Phone | Email | LinkedIn (centered, one line)
- Title banner: ROLE TITLE | Credential 1 | Credential 2 | Credential 3 (bold, below contact)
- Section headers: Colored (teal/blue), underlined — e.g. "Professional summary", "Education", "Professional experience"
- Company names: Bold, underlined
- Role titles: Italic
- Dates: Right-aligned
- Bullet points: • (filled circle), indented, concise action-oriented language
- Page: Single page preferred; tight spacing

## MANDATORY SECTION ORDER

### 1. HEADER
```
INON BAASOV
+972-54-444-5856 | Inonbaasov@gmail.com | LinkedIn inonbaasov
[ROLE TITLE] | B.Sc. Engineering | Executive MBA | [Track-specific credential]
```

### 2. PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY
- 3–5 sentences
- Opens with seniority + years + domain
- Includes 2–3 key metrics inline (e.g. $2.5M, 38%, 22%)
- Ends with value proposition or passion statement
- **KEY SKILLS line** directly below summary paragraph:
  `Key Skills: Skill 1 | Skill 2 | Skill 3 | Skill 4 | Skill 5 | Skill 6 | Skill 7`

### 3. PROFESSIONAL EXPERIENCE
Format per role:
```
[Company Name] — [Industry descriptor]          [Start Year] – [End Year]
[Role Title / Promoted Title]
• Bullet 1 — lead with strongest achievement
• Bullet 2
• Bullet 3
```
Rules:
- Most recent role first (reverse chronological)
- Each role must LEAD INTO the next — career narrative thread must be visible
- Promotion within same company shown as sub-roles
- Use bold for key terms/metrics within bullets
- 3–6 bullets per primary role
- Earlier/less-relevant roles: 1–2 lines max, or grouped under "Previous Experience"

### 4. EDUCATION
```
• [Year] [Degree], [Field] — [Institution]
• [Year] [Degree], [Field] — [Institution]
• [Year] [Degree], [Field] — [Institution]
```

### 5. CONDITIONAL SECTIONS (job-offer dependent)
Include ONLY if relevant to the specific job offer:
- **Military Service** — include if: leadership, ops, government/defense, structured environments
- **Medical/Pharma Experience** — 3 options:
  a. Full inclusion: if role requires regulated industry, QA, compliance, MedTech
  b. Abbreviated (1–2 lines): if tangentially relevant
  c. Omit entirely: if completely irrelevant to role
  - Can be reframed as: regulatory expertise, quality systems, compliance, manufacturing, or process management
- **Portfolio** (when ready): add as a section with product links
